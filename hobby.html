About Knitting

My Favorite Hobby

Knitting is a creative hobby that uses two needles and yarn to make fabric items like sweaters, scarves, hats, and blankets. To start, you'll need yarn, knitting needles, scissors, and a measuring tape. The basic stitches are the knit stitch (simple pattern) and the purl stitch (different texture). Knitting is relaxing, reduces stress, and lets you express creativity with colors and patterns. Beginners should start with simple projects like scarves and practice regularly to improve.
